Welcome to Knight & Rose

Whatever your figure or dress dreams, I can design the perfect garment for you, creating something individual that reflects your personality and flatters your figure at a price you can afford. Every dress I create is unique, and with hundreds of fabric samples for you to peruse, you can have a dress that really is like no other, and will fit you perfectly without having to pay for alterations on top of a costly dress from a bridal boutique store. I also design and make matching bags, ties and cravats, waistcoats, bridesmaid dresses, veils, mother of the bride outfits, and other accessories. Contact me for a no obligation initial appointment to discuss your dress needs and desires.
